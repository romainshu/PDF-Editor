Search

Pattern (amber) — default finds 6 letters/digits (≥1 digit) + 1 trailing letter, e.g. 1234b6T. Use the preset dropdown for common patterns (names, emails, phone numbers, SSN-style) or type your own. Click an amber highlight to copy it
Literal text (pink) — finds the exact word/phrase you type, no regex needed. Click a pink highlight to copy it
Signature (blue) — finds the word "Signature"; highlight only, shows total count and pages
Fillable blanks (green) — finds underscore lines like ______; click a green highlight to drop a text box right on it, ready to type
"Also OCR scanned pages" extends every search above to pages with no real text layer (scanned/photographed documents) — slower, off by default
